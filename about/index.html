About

About me and my skill set

Hey there! I'm Laura Kam, a Junior Frontend Developer with a passion for creating awesome digital experiences. I specialize in designing aesthetically pleasing apps that not only look great but also prioritize seamless usability. I have a solid knowledge of HTML, CSS, CSS3, SASS, JavaScript, TypeScript, UX/UI, and technical SEO, I also hold a Full Stack Development certificate from the Univeristy of Birmingham.

My motivation

Well, it all started with my own experiences using various apps in my personal and work life. I couldn't help but notice areas where improvements could be made. Coding has allowed me to problem solve while making full use of my creative side.

My work background

Before venturing into the world of development, I spent over five years as a teacher. This background has equipped me with valuable project management, communication, critical thinking, and adaptability skills. I'm detail-oriented, goal-driven, and take immense pride in my work.

My personality, interests and hobbies

My colleagues have described me as primarily, conscienscious, committed, a curious, independent learner, community-minded and enthusiastic about the work I do.
